Threshold Sweep Experiment

The classifier decision threshold was varied to observe how
detection behavior changes.

Thresholds tested:
0.05, 0.10, 0.20, 0.30, 0.50, 0.70, 0.90

Findings

Lower thresholds:
- increase detection rates
- increase alert volume (false positives)

Higher thresholds:
- reduce alerts
- increase missed attacks

This demonstrates the classic IDS tradeoff between
detection sensitivity and operational workload.
